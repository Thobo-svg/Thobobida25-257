How to Apply

1

Enquiry

Contact us via email or visit our offices to learn about available programmes and intake dates.

2

Submit Documents

Please provide the following:

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school reports
  • Parent/Guardian ID
  • Passport-size photos
3

Assessment

Students may sit a short placement assessment to ensure they are placed in the correct grade level.

4

Enrolment

Once accepted, complete the enrolment forms and pay the registration fee to secure your place.
